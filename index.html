Plastic Pollution

8 million tons of plastic leak into the ocean every year.
  • 1,000,000 marine animals die due to plastic pollution every year.
  • About 91% of plastic is not recycled.

Why This Matters

  • Plastic takes around 500-1000 years to decompose and during that
    time poses a threat to wildlife and the environment.
  • Plastics break down into microplastics that works its way up the food chain
    and into our bodies.

What You Can Do

  • Use reusable items instead of disposable, single-use items.
  • Always recycle your plastics when throwing them away.
